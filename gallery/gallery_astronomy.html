India's Astronomy Legacy

Aryabhata

Aryabhata introduced the concept of Earth’s rotation on its axis. He proposed corrections for planetary speeds in the sky and calculated the sidereal rotation as 23 hours, 56 minutes, and 4.1 seconds...

Varahamihira

Varahamihira was a pioneer in Indian astronomy. His work, the Pancha-Siddhantika, integrated knowledge from Greek, Egyptian, and Indian astronomy. He also emphasized weather prediction and planetary motion...

Brahmagupta

Brahmagupta contributed extensively to celestial mechanics and planetary calculations. He explained lunar phases, solar eclipses, and systematic methods to calculate planetary longitudes...
