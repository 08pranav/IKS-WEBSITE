500 BCE

Panini

Authored Ashtadhyayi, the earliest known work on grammar and linguistics. Introduced formal rules and concepts of computational thinking.

500 BCE

Nyaya Sutras

Developed by Gautama, the Nyaya Sutras laid the foundation for Indian logic and epistemology, influencing philosophical reasoning.
