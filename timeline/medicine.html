Medicine and Surgery

1200–1000 BCE

Atharvaveda

Early mentions of herbal medicine and treatments, highlighting natural remedies for illnesses.

300 BCE

Charaka Samhita

Foundational text of Ayurveda written by Charaka. Discussed physiology, diagnosis, and treatments for various diseases.

600 BCE

Sushruta Samhita

Sushruta, the “Father of Surgery,” described over 300 surgical procedures, including reconstructive surgery and cataract removal techniques.

6th Century BCE

Plastic Surgery in India

Documented as a treatment for nasal injuries and deformities, showcasing India's advanced surgical techniques.
