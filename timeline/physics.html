Physics and Chemistry

600 BCE

Kanada

Authored the Vaisheshika Sutra, theorizing about atoms (anu) and molecules. Suggested that matter is made of indivisible particles.

2nd Century CE

Nagarjuna

Advanced theories on alchemy and metallurgy. Wrote Rasaratnakara, detailing the use of metals in medicine and chemical processes.
