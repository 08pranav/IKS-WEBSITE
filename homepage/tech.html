Technology - Ancient India's Achievements

Key achievements in Technology

Metallurgy - Wootz Steel

Ancient India pioneered the art of metallurgy with the development of Wootz steel. It was a high-quality steel known for its strength and durability, used to make weapons like swords.

Water Management - Stepwells

Stepwells were an innovative solution for water storage and management, particularly in dry regions. They were deep wells with steps leading down to the water level, providing access to water during hot months.

Ancient Textiles

Ancient India was known for its advanced textile industry. Indian fabrics such as muslin, silk, and cotton were highly prized and traded worldwide.
