Medicine - Ancient India's Achievements

Key Achievements in Medicine

Charaka

Charaka was one of the principal contributors to the development of Ayurveda. His work on the Charaka Samhita provided a comprehensive guide to diagnosing and treating various diseases, emphasizing the use of natural remedies and lifestyle changes for maintaining health.

Sushruta

Sushruta is often referred to as the 'father of surgery.' His text, the Sushruta Samhita, is one of the oldest and most detailed texts on surgery. He is known for pioneering surgical techniques such as cataract removal and plastic surgery.

Herbal Remedies

Ancient Indian medicine extensively utilized herbal remedies for various ailments. The use of plants such as Ashwagandha, Tulsi, and Neem for healing and prevention was a key aspect of Ayurveda.
